Ayanah's Amazing Mexican Breakfast Burrito

When you hear breakfast burrito you probably think greasy bacon, loads of scrambled egg and lots of heavy cheese.

I know some people love that verion, but my version is a plant-based spin off, with tofu scramble, refried beans, salsa and veggie sour cream in a torilla. (YUM, veggies can be delicious)

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla of your choice
  • Salsa of your choice (peach is my fav)
  • Sour cream or cream cheese (I use tofuttie brand)
  • Refried beans
  • 1 cup kale
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup red onion
  • Tofu scramble (requires a lot of ingredients):
    1. 1/5 package tofu
    2. 1 table spoon nutritional yeast
    3. 1/4 tablespoon tumeric
    4. 1/4 tablespoon cumin
    5. 1/4 tablespoon garlic powder
    6. 1 teaspoon salt

Lets cook! Steps:

  • Put oven on to 350, and put a greased pan on medium on the stove top.
  • Open your can of refried beans and put on tortilla and pop in heating oven. (this will stay in here untill the fillings are ready)
  • Break up tofu pieces into pretty small pieces and put in bowl with seasonigs. Shake untill covered them put in heated pan for 6-8 minutes or until hot and yellow. (The color and look is very remminicent of scrambled eggs.)
  • While waiting for the tofu to cook, and tortilla/beans to heat prepare your veggies.
  • Take heated tortilla with beans out of the ovean and add the prepared kale, tomatoes and red onion.
  • Add the tofu scramble ontop with the salsa and sour cream.
  • Finally..... ENJOYYY NOMNOMNOM !
