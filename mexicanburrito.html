Ayanah's Amazing Mexican Breakfast Burrito

Here is a plant-based spin off of the classic breakfast burrito. It has delicious tofu scramble, refried beans, veggies, salsa and veggie sour cream all wrapped up in a torilla. (YUM!!!!)

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla of your choice
  • Salsa of your choice (peach is my fav)
  • Sour cream or cream cheese
  • Refried beans
  • 1 cup kale
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup red onion
  • Tofu scramble:
    1. 1/5 package tofu
    2. 1 table spoon nutritional yeast
    3. 1/4 tablespoon tumeric
    4. 1/4 tablespoon cumin
    5. 1/4 tablespoon garlic powder
    6. 1 teaspoon salt

Lets cook! Steps:

  • Put oven on to 350 and put a greased pan on medium heat on the stove top.
  • Open your can of refried beans and put 2-3 spoons fulls on tortilla. When this is all spread out evenly pop it in the heating oven. (this will stay in until the fillings are ready)
  • Break up tofu pieces into pretty small pieces and put into bowl with all of the seasonigs. Mix or shake until the tofu is completely covered. Then pour into heated pan for 6-8 minutes or until hot and yellow making sure to occasionaly stir. (The color and look is very remminicent of scrambled eggs.)
  • While waiting for the tofu to cook, and tortilla/beans to heat prepare your veggies. Cut up tomatoes, onion and kale.
  • Take heated tortilla with beans out of the oven and add the prepared kale, tomatoes and red onion.
  • Add the tofu scramble ontop and finish with the salsa and sour cream.
  • Finally..... ENJOYYY NOMNOMNOM !
