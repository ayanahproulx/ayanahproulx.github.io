Ayanah's Yummy Pancake Recipe Card

Ingredients:

1 large ripe banana, 1 cup oats, 2 cups soy milk, ½ cup flour, 2 tsp baking powder, pinch of salt and any delicious toppings choco chips, blueberries, etc.)

Time:

20 minutes in total; 10 minutes prep, 10 minutes of cooking

Directions:

  • Mash ripe bananas
  • Mix mashed bananas to other pre-measured ingredients
  • Heat pan over medium heat and spray with oil
  • Scoop half cup amounts of batter into pan to form a pancake
  • Wait until 2-4 minutes or until bubbles form and flip
  • Repeat untill batter is gone
  • Plate with any yummy toppings and add lots of maple syrup or coconut whip
