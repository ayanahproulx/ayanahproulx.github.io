Incredible GF and Vegan Breakfast Burrito

Total Time
20 min
Prep
10 min
Ingredients
Directions

Ingredients ↓

  • ⅓ cup refried beans
  • 1 large brown rice tortilla
  • 1 cup kale
  • ¼ cup red onion
  • ¼ cup tomatoe
  • ¼ cup salsa of your choice (I love peach salsa the best!
  • 2 tbsp vegan sour cream or vegan cream cheese
  • Tofu scramble which requires:
  • 1 serving firm tofu, 2 tbsp nutritional yeast, 1 teaspoon cumin, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp tumeric and 1 tbsp garlic salt.

Directions ↓

  1. First preheat oven to 350 then heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.

  2. Spread refried beans onto torilla and put in oven.

  3. Break tofu into quarter size crumbles in bowl and add seasonings. Mix well before dumping in hot pan.

  4. Chop up red onion, tomatoe and kale into bite-size pieces, while occasionally stiring tofu in pan.

  5. Add veggies to pan and stir for 4-5 minutes, or until hot.

  6. Take out warmed tortilla from oven, and top with tofu and veggie mixture from the pan.

  7. Top with salsa and sour cream.

  8. Don't forget to enjoy and keep eatin goood
